# How do I fix a Kali Linux resolution?

import Callout from 'nextra-theme-docs/callout'

First go to all apps and search for “Settings Manager”.

![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/85288261/204073122-1181d1eb-cd0d-428a-8008-8119cc6a2007.png)

Then the “Setting” window will open.

![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/85288261/204073125-1839060c-c3d6-45d1-915a-a64ccff2114a.png)

Here click on “Display” on “Hardware” menu.

Now we will get the display section. Here you can change your display resolution as per your display.

![image](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/85288261/204073136-a1682e05-1a12-4716-a3b8-48ad6aa45f25.png)

When display is perfectly fits you can click on “Apply” to save it.

> That’s it. Hope this helps you.
